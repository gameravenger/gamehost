For Users/Players

How do I register for a game?

1. Create an account and login

2. Browse games and select one you want to join

3. Choose your sheets and make payment via UPI

4. Enter UTR ID for verification

5. Wait for organiser approval

6. Download your sheets once approved

My payment is not getting approved. What should I do?

Contact the game organiser directly via WhatsApp (available on game page) with your UTR ID and payment screenshot. If still not resolved, contact our support.

I can't download my sheets. Why?

Sheets can only be downloaded after organiser approves your payment. Check your payment status in your dashboard or contact the organiser.
