  • Fill in your details: Username, Email, Mobile Number, and Password
  • Accept terms and conditions

  • Click on a game to view details

  • Select specific sheet numbers from the grid
  • Review your selection and total amount

Make Payment

Payment Process

  • Scan the QR code with any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Pay the exact amount shown
  • Note down the UTR/Transaction ID from your payment app
  • Enter the UTR ID in the verification form
  • Enter the mobile number used for payment
  • Click "Submit for Verification"
üí≥ Payment Tips:
  • Pay only through the provided QR code
  • Double-check the amount before paying
  • Keep your UTR ID safe for verification
  • Payment goes directly to the organiser

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my payment is not verified?

+

Contact the organiser directly via WhatsApp with your payment screenshot and UTR ID. They will manually verify and approve your participation.

Can I get a refund if I can't join?

+

Refund policies are set by individual organisers. Contact the organiser before the game starts to discuss refund options.

What if I miss downloading my sheets?

+

Contact the organiser immediately. They may provide alternative access, but it's not guaranteed. Always download sheets as soon as you're approved.

How do I know if I won?

+

Winners are announced during the live game and later updated on the leaderboard. Prize distribution details are provided by the organiser.

Is my money safe?

+

Payments go directly to verified organisers. GameBlast Mobile is a platform service. Always verify organiser details and read our disclaimer before participating.
