GameBlast Mobile is a SaaS platform that facilitates gaming services. We do not directly handle money transactions between users and organisers. All payments are made directly to game organisers via UPI.

1. Platform Service Refunds

As GameBlast Mobile is a Software as a Service (SaaS) platform, we provide the following refund policies:

1.1 User Services

  • Platform usage is free for users/players
  • No charges are made by GameBlast Mobile to users
  • All game participation fees are paid directly to organisers

1.2 Organiser Monthly Fees

  • Monthly organiser fee of ‚Çπ2,500 is non-refundable once paid
  • Fee covers platform access, technical support, and infrastructure
  • No partial refunds for unused portions of the month
  • Account suspension or termination does not entitle refund

2. Game Participation Refunds

For game participation fees paid to organisers:

2.1 Direct Payment Policy

  • All game fees are paid directly to organisers via UPI
  • GameBlast Mobile does not process or hold these payments
  • Refund requests must be made directly to the game organiser
  • Platform provides contact information for organiser communication

2.2 Organiser Refund Responsibilities

  • Organisers are responsible for their own refund policies
  • Organisers must clearly state their refund terms before game start
  • Platform encourages fair refund practices by organisers
  • Disputes should be resolved directly between user and organiser

3. Refund Request Process

3.1 For Platform Services (Organiser Fees)

In exceptional circumstances, organiser fee refunds may be considered:

  • Technical platform failure preventing service usage
  • Duplicate payment due to system error
  • Unauthorized payment with proof

Process:

  1. Contact support within 7 days of payment
  2. Provide payment proof and detailed explanation
  3. Allow 7-14 business days for review
  4. Refunds processed to original payment method

3.2 For Game Participation

For game-related refunds:

  1. Contact the game organiser directly via WhatsApp/phone
  2. Reference your UTR ID and game details
  3. If unresolved, contact platform support for mediation
  4. Platform may suspend organisers with excessive complaints

4. Non-Refundable Situations

4.1 Platform Services

  • Change of mind after organiser approval
  • Account violations leading to suspension
  • Failure to use platform services
  • Technical issues on user's device
  • Internet connectivity problems

4.2 Game Participation

  • Games completed as scheduled
  • User's failure to join live games
  • Technical issues on user's end
  • Disputes with other participants
  • Dissatisfaction with game results

5. Dispute Resolution

5.1 Platform Mediation

GameBlast Mobile provides mediation services for:

  • Payment verification disputes
  • Organiser-user conflicts
  • Technical platform issues
  • Service quality complaints

5.2 Escalation Process

  1. Direct Resolution: Contact organiser directly
  2. Platform Support: Contact our support team
  3. Formal Complaint: Submit detailed complaint with evidence
  4. Review Process: 7-14 days investigation period
  5. Final Decision: Platform's decision is final

6. Organiser Obligations

6.1 Refund Policy Disclosure

  • Must clearly state refund policy before game registration
  • Cannot change refund terms after users have paid
  • Must honor stated refund commitments
  • Must respond to refund requests within 48 hours

6.2 Fair Practice Requirements

  • Cannot refuse legitimate refund requests
  • Must provide refunds for cancelled games
  • Must refund if technical issues prevent game completion
  • Violation may result in platform suspension
